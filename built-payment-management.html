Payment Management Platform

Built Technologies

Role
End-to-end experience design for developers, general contractors, and subcontractors. Collaborated with PM on product strategy, designed and shipped multiple features validated through user research.
Impact
Subcontractors got paid in 27 days vs. 55-day industry average (51% faster). $290M processed across 6,000+ organizations in 18 months. Payment management became the #1 feature in sales demos. Solved "unsolvable" lower-tier lien waiver collection problem. Created shared platform language across entire construction payment chain.

When I joined Built, they'd spent a decade focused on lenders. Contractor and subcontractor experiences were designed in silos, creating fragmentation. Through mental modeling, I uncovered the Built Chain insight: developers, contractors, and subs shared nearly identical jobs (collect docs, package payment requests, get paid, pay it forward).

I aligned these experiences so Built could move faster by reusing shared functionality, and users could speak the same visual and operational language when collaborating. The breakthrough: we could decouple documents from requirements, giving every stakeholder instant visibility instead of waiting weeks for paper to move up the chain.

"Before Built, we spent 1–2 hours tracking down compliance docs and cutting checks. Now we can use that time to focus on project financials and see what's going on." Melissa Loftin, Project Accountant at Waltz Construction
"Once subcontractors see it work flawlessly the first time and get their money faster, they're happy we switched." Lisa Augusto, Project Accountant
