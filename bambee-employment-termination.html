Bambee Employment Termination Workflow

Role
  • Sole designer
  • Led research with HR managers, designed and shipped the triage flows with state-specific compliance gates
Impact
  • 95% of terminations became self-service, freeing HR managers to focus on the hardest 5%
  • Turned around Bambee's NPS score
  • CTO: "The end of employment feature turned around our NPS score."

Small business owners feared legal exposure. One wrong move could put their livelihood at risk. I shadowed Bambee's HR managers and partnered with their compliance expert to bottle their expertise into a self-service system.

The insight: some clients tried to soften a firing by calling it a layoff, not realizing the legal risk that created. I designed a three-layer triage (safety → compliance → emotion) that routed high-risk cases to human support while enabling routine terminations to happen confidently without waiting.

"The end of employment feature turned around our NPS score."

—Mike Ruangutai, former CTO of Bambee
