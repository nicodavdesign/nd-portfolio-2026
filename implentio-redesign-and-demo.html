App Redesign + Vibe-Coded Demo

Implentio

Role
Built complete front-end application using Figma Make and Claude AI as a solo designer in a seed-stage startup
Impact
Freed 2-3 weeks of engineering to focus on onboarding (the actual bottleneck blocking contracted revenue). Sales now demos with current workflows, authentic data, and production-ready interactions. Built in 100 hours using Figma Make + Claude AI. Created 6 new reusable React components extending design system.

I'd redesigned Implentio's core reconciliation workflows and evolved the design system. Our sales demo was months out of date, still showing MVP workflows our customers found confusing. Engineering was at capacity onboarding new clients with massive datasets. My CTO and Chief Customer Officer saw an opportunity: what if design could start to own the front-end independently, keeping engineering focused on core work?

I built the demo using Figma Make and Claude AI. I designed directly in the interface, solving problems engineers usually handle: state management, local persistence, dynamic filters, bulk updates, proper back navigation. I created seven invoices across three types and 61 orders with realistic line items, dates, and error patterns that actually add up. It feels production-ready because it practically is.

"It's certainly more intuitive. Not having seen it before, I naturally fell into the flow of things." Crystal, customer describing the new IA and error groups
